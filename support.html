FAQ

How do I change the reminder interval?
Tap the gear icon → Select a preset or use the +/- buttons.
Is my data synced to the cloud?
No. All data is stored locally on your device for privacy.
How do I enable notifications?
Go to Settings → Donema → Notifications → Allow.
